# RC2014 Newsletter Archive — Issue Summaries

Brief summaries of RC2014's newsletter, newest first. This is a plain reference list, not part of the wiki.

Sources:
- Mailchimp archive (issues 49–68, Nov 2023 – Jun 2026): https://us13.campaign-archive.com/home/?u=9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd&id=21fa309286
- Official archive (issues 1–50 + Easter Special, 2016–2024): https://rc2014.co.uk/history/rc2014-newsletter-archive/

Issue 1 (mid-2016) is the earliest known issue.

### Issue 68 — 2026-06-25
[Read issue](https://mailchi.mp/ad8022dd8ebf/rc2014-newsletter-issue-6764396?e=bc8e1510ad)

Announces the RC2014 Zed Mini (compact form factor with 512k RAM/ROM) launching by end of June, and the ZBot80 robot buggy launching next month, programmable via BASIC or Assembly. Previews RC2014 Assembly 2.0 on September 26, 2026 at The National Museum of Computing, plus EMF 2026 (limited-edition Captain Zilog magazines) and a sold-out RetroFest 2026. Also covers Shiela Dixon's Orton 3C music project and a 15% expansion module discount (code EXPAN-15).

### Issue 67 — 2026-04-21
[Read issue](https://us13.campaign-archive.com/?u=9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd&id=66687863e1)

Announces the new Zed II and Zed Pro II kits, promotes Retrofest 2026 and Electromagnetic Field 2026, and highlights Dr. Scott M. Baker's NostOS operating system release and Shiela Dixon's Orton 3C etch-a-sketch project. Includes a £3 discount on the 39SF040 ROM programmer and notes Tindie marketplace downtime.

### Issue 66 — 2026-03-05
[Read issue](https://us13.campaign-archive.com/?u=9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd&id=00e985ad54)
