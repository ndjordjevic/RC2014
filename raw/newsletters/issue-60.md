## Limited Edition RC2014 Mini II Picasso

After months of work, the limited edition [RC2014 Mini II Picasso](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii-picasso/>) can finally be unveiled. Based on the RC2014 Mini II this work of art has thrown the rule book out of the window and dared to do things that others wouldn't even dream of.  
Straight lines and right angles are only for squares. The Mini II Picasso doesn't do things conventionally. It is, however, still 100% compatible with all Mini II expansions, including the[ Mini II CP/M Upgrade Kit](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii-cp-m-upgrade-kit/>).   
|  |  ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/62ea2869-5465-9d90-4dbf-a6e5e085dd19.jpg)  
---  
Available in 4 PCB colours, every one of the 100 kits available will have a unique combination of PCB, header socket, LED and barrel jack colours. The Perspex enclosure is included and even has a keyhole in the back for hanging in an art gallery.   
---  
|  ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/f5b18058-0d0a-2275-8097-05bc75cfb5dd.jpg)  
---  
These kits use through hole parts. Literally. Including a chip that is fitted through a hole, and spiral resistor staircases that even share a hole! These kits have been likened to [Tommy Coopers](<https://youtu.be/L9UMvfKBaZI>) bad magic tricks or [Les Dawsons](<https://youtu.be/LQ0G2Z8k1Gg>) terrible piano playing.

## Retrofest 2025 - Swindon May 31st June 1st

Short Circuit Events, the team behind the RCF in Cambridge, are launching the inaugural [Retrofest](<https://www.retrofest.uk/>) in Swindon at the end of May. This promises to be a fanstatic event, and RC2014 will be well represented there. Grab a ticket early to avoid disappointment.
