## CP/M Upgrade Kit for the RC2014 Mini

  
If you have a [RC2014 Mini](https://www.tindie.com/products/semachthemonkey/rc2014-mini-single-board-z80-computer-kit/) and feel that you're missing out on the exciting world of CP/M, then worry no longer! This [CP/M Upgrade Kit](https://www.tindie.com/products/semachthemonkey/rc2014-mini-cpm-upgrade-kit/) combines the features of the Pageable ROM Module, 64k RAM Module, and Compact Flash Module, as well as offering 2 expansion slots. No modifications to the RC2014 Mini are needed, and surface mount compact flash socket comes pre-soldered!

## The National Museum of Computing

Based at Bletchly Park, and home to the oldest working digital computer, [TNMOC](https://www.tnmoc.org/) is in need of [a little support](https://www.tnmoc.org/make-a-donation). Like many places, it has been hit hard by the Covid restrictions, but is now open for tours. If you can't make it in person, the [virtual tour](https://www.tnmoc.org/3d-virtual-tour) is fantastic too.
