## Emulated YM2149 / AY-3-8910 Sounds Great

Keeping up with a new module every month, the [Why Em-Ulator Sound Module](<https://z80kits.com/shop/why-em-ulator-sound-module/>) has just launched. This emulated YM2149 sound card means that you don't have to take your chances with used chips on eBay and still get that vintage chip tune experience. And if you don't like to see modern SMD components on your RC2014, we've got you covered. Literally. With an optional DIP40 socket.   
|  |  ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/4b7d1756-0ba8-0bfe-849e-ae63f0ff8664.jpg)  
---  
The addressing has been simplified so this works with existing RC2014 demos and the PLAYER.COM program as well as easily switchable for MSX ports and TMS9918A compatibility.

## In The Dark RC2014

“[In The Dark](<https://github.com/kianryan/InTheDark/>)” is a rogue-like game by [Kyan Ryan](<https://www.kianryan.co.uk>) that sees you playing as an explorer of caves. Find the treasures in each level, and move on to the next. But the more you move, the more your light diminishes, and Grue lurk in the cave with you. The Grue don’t like the light, but they love the dark and they also love the taste of your bones. Available for your [CP/M RC2014](<https://github.com/kianryan/InTheDark/releases>)
