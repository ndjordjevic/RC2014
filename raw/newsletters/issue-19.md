## What European RC2014 Customers Need To Know

I've been holding back on this newsletter for a few weeks now, as I wanted to provide some clarification about what's going on with Brexit, and how that will affect RC2014 shipments to the European Union.  I wish I could offer some clarification now, however things have been changing day by day for the last month.  Official advice to me has been contradictorary, and even those that should know what's going on still seem unclear.  
However, the way it looks at the moment is that the UK will not leave the EU without a deal, and that the current deadline of this Friday will probably be extended.  
This is good news as it looks like for the foreseeable future, RC2014 customers in the EU will not have to pay any customs charges, tax or import duty on RC2014 kits.  
If that does change, I will do my best to let you know before any charges come in to effect.
