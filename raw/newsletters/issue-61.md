## New Snake in Town

Some of you may already know Niels Haedecke as the sysop of RC-Box (rc2014.ddns.net:2014), currently the only BBS hosted on an RC2014. Well, he is also a Forth programmer, and has written [TMSnake](<https://megagumbo.itch.io/tmsnake>) for the RC2014, on an RC2014!   
|  |  [ ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/486355b8-202e-f09a-fffb-3faadcec229e.jpg) ](<https://www.tindie.com/products/shieladixon/tmsemu3-graphics-card-designed-for-rc2014/>)  
---  
As the name suggests, it runs on a TMS9918A video card or compatible such as the [TMSEMU3](<https://www.tindie.com/products/shieladixon/tmsemu3-graphics-card-designed-for-rc2014/>). It also has support for [AY-3-8910 ](<https://z80kits.com/shop/why-em-ulator-sound-module/>)based sound cards and the [Tynemouth Joystick Module](<https://z80kits.com/shop/tynemouth-joystick-module-for-rc2014/>).  
  
This really is a good version of the classic Snake game, and provided some good competetive fun here at RC2014 Towers. Can you beat the high score of 460 set by Yours Truly?

## Coming Soon - Minstrel 4th

Fans of the Jupiter Ace and of Tynemouth kits will be pleased to know that the Minstrel 4th will soon be available at z80kits. This recreation of the Jupiter Ace is an excellent way for people to get in to the FORTH programming language. Along with Jupiter Ace compatibility, this kit offers extra memory, different display options as well as... an RC2014 expansion bus!

## Easter Egg!

Easter means Easter Eggs to many of us, and this year there was an extra Easter Egg for everybody that has an [RC2014 Mini II Picasso](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii-picasso/>). Did you find your Easter Egg?
