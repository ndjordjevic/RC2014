## RC2014 Mini II and Mini II CP/M Upgrade Kit

The RC2014 [Mini II](https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii/) and the RC2014 [Mini II CP/M Upgrade Kit](https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii-cp-m-upgrade-kit/) are now available on z80kits. These kits build on the great form factor of the original Mini, but add some great features, such as   
|  |  ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2048_files/2ff7df42-7a01-5eaf-8b18-6f0272f0fd2c.jpg)  
---  
Enhanced Bus support, 8k or 16k pageable ROM, a 128k ROM loaded with various options to boot to different versions of BASIC, SCM, CP/M and even FORTH.  
The Mini II or the Mini II with CP/M Upgrade have both been designed to work with an optional laser cut enclosure which still allows access to the jumpers you need to get to.

## Minstrel from Tynemouth
