## RC2014 Assembly - 20th September 2025

Apart from last months newsletter, and on [social media](<https://oldbytes.space/@rc2014/115063159960273773>), and the [RC2014-Z80 Google Group](<https://groups.google.com/g/rc2014-z80/c/FcV7fAzhClw/m/i5g3-fA2CgAJ>) and the [RC2014 website](<https://rc2014.co.uk/3855/rc2014-assembly-v1-0/>), have I mentioned the upcoming RC2014 Assembly on September 20th? That's less than a month away!  
There are still some tables left for exhibitors and traders, so if you have cool RC2014, RCBus, Z50 Bus or other nice modern retro computers to show off or sell kits of, please book a ticket now.  
A few people have already expressed an interest in giving a 10-15 minute lightning talk, but more talks are always welcome.  
   
|  |  [ ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/861c92ee-b918-46be-af3d-0b6a851d48aa.png) ](<https://www.tindie.com/products/shieladixon/tmsemu3-graphics-card-designed-for-rc2014/>)  
---  
Tickets for exhibitors and traders are limited, but you will get a very special RC2014 badge!  
  
If you're not sure what to exhibit or demo on the day, feel free to [contact me](<mailto:spencer@z80kits.com?subject=RC2014%20Assembly>) if you want to discuss your ideas.  
  
Tickets are available on [Eventbrite](<https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/rc2014-assembly-tickets-1444643490569>)

## Sneak peek 

Available at the end of August, the [RC2014 Orton 3C](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-orton-3c/>) will appeal to the hardcore Z80 users. Featuring a front panel for programming, 32k RAM, 4MHz CPU and serial I/O - all achieved with just 3 chips! Add in a Port 0 I/O module and there's hours of toggling and LED fun!
