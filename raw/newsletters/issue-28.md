## Hardware Multiply Module

In order to eek as much speed as possible out of the humble Z80, Phillip Stevens has designed a [LUT Module](https://groups.google.com/d/msg/rc2014-z80/Oly0f1qwZv8/2e4kFiBCEwAJ) which speeds up the RC2014 by offloading the multiplication work. This has reduced the time taken to draw the classic [Mandlebrot](https://github.com/RC2014Z80/RC2014/tree/master/BASIC-Programs/Mandlebrot) from 4'58 down to 2'55.  
If you want to boost your RC2014 speed, the PCBs have been made available over at [OSHPark](https://oshpark.com/shared_projects/AFMxXQVZ)
