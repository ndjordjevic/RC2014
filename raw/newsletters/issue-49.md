As I'm sure you know from your inbox, it will soon be Black Friday. Love it or hate it there is no getting away from this annual event. ## TMSEMU - An emulated TMS9918A 

The TMS9918A is a great graphics chip, but is limited by composite video output and no serial console. These have been addressed by Shiela Dixon with her [TMSEMU](<https://hackaday.io/project/191880-emulated-tms9918a-using-rp2040dvi>) that is compatible with existing TMS code but boasts crisp HDMI video and basic serial support. The first batch sold out but there is a wait list on [Tindie](<https://www.tindie.com/products/shieladixon/tmsemu-graphics-card-designed-for-rc2014/>) to sign up for notification when they are back in stock.
