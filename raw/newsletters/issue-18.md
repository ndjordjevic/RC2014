## Hackaday Circuit Sculpture Contest

When [Matseng](https://twitter.com/matseng) came to assemble his RC2014 kit, he realised that ditching the PCBs would allow him to enter the [Circuit Sculpture Contest](https://blog.tindie.com/2019/01/circuit-sculpture-contest-winners/) at Hackaday - which he won! See more details [here](https://hackaday.io/project/162910-wirez80)  
Congratulations Mats, it's beautiful!
