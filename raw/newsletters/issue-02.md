# 64K RAM Module

One of the more exciting, and much asked for modules is the new [64k RAM Module](https://www.tindie.com/products/Semachthemonkey/64k-ram-module-for-rc2014-z80-homebrew-computer/).  The lower 32k can be toggled on or off, allowing it to coexist with ROM, or take up the memory left over from a 4k, 8k or 16k ROM  
  
To blatantly misquote Bill Gates "64k should be enough for anyone!"

# Retro Challenge 2016/10

The RC2014 started life as en entry in the Retro Challenge.  Well, the [Retro Challenge](http://retrochallenge.net/) is back this October.  I have [entered](http://www.wickensonline.co.uk/retrochallenge-2012sc/rc201610-entrants-list/) as usual, and this time continuing the challenge of making a ZX81 module for the RC2014 - which I ran out of time for last time around.  
In a great "Inception" like twist, [Scott](http://www.wintergroundfairlands.com/2016/06/the-rc2014-computer-1-emulation.html) is also in the challenge, and basing his around the RC2014!  Good luck Scott!  And good luck to everyone else too.  There's always an amazing amount of interesting stuff going on by some very tallented people  
There's still time to enter if you get yourself over to <http://retrochallenge.net/>
