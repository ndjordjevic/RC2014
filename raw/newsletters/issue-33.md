## Shipping times

Shipping times are always a bit stretched at this time of the year, and for most of 2020 they haven't been great either. Predictions are that the postal systems will be busier than ever this year. All RC2014 kits now have a tracked option, so although it won't get them to you any quicker, at least it is peace of mind knowing where they are.
