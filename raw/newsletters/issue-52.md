## SID-Ulator Sound Module

  
Following on from last months launch of the [Why Em-Ulator Sound Module](<https://z80kits.com/shop/why-em-ulator-sound-module/>), the [SID-Ulator Sound Module ](<https://z80kits.com/shop/sid-ulator-sound-module/>)brings emulated SID music to your RC2014. Easily programmable, even from BASIC, this recreates some of the most iconic sounds of 80s home computing with a nano SwinSID hiding under the dummy 28 DIP chip.

## EU Shipping and VAT charges

From April 1st, **z80kits will no longer be pre-charging VAT for orders to European Union** countries. This allows for orders to be sent by Royal Mail and will result in huge savings on shipping.  VAT charges will still apply, but these will be collected and processed by the local postal service. Read more about the decision making process [here](<https://z80kits.com/ioss-eu-shipping-and-z80kits/>)
