# Happy New Year!

  
Just a very quick news letter today to wish everybody a fantastic 2017 and to say thanks for your support over 2016.  The last 12 months have certainly been amazing and have taken RC2014 kit sales from 25 to well over 500!  
  
I can't find sales figures for other Z80 based computers, but I think that probably makes the RC2014 the **biggest selling Z80 computer of 2016!**  I can't thank you enough for helping to make that happen!  
  
![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%204_files/072ac38e-7cf3-433c-99d7-211492b75d75.jpg)  
The community support has been fantastic with so many of you helping others out, sharing their ideas and software, and helping to spread the word.  Special mention should go to Filippo Bergamasco, Scott Lawrence, Jenny List, Scott Baker , Phillip Stevens and Ken Boak for helping get the RC2014 and its community to where it is today.  Thank you.  
  
And, of course, no RC2014 news letter would be complete without a discount code, so if you go to [Tindie](https://www.tindie.com/stores/Semachthemonkey/) before 19th January and use the code **TWENTY17** at the checkout, you will get 17% off of any RC2014 product!  How's that for a good start to the year?
