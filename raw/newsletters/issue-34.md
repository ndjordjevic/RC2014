## Brexit and EU import duty

  
As December 31 gets closer, it is looking less likely that the UK and EU will agree a trade deal. This means that there will probably be import duty and tax applied to RC2014 kits going in to Europe. Currently most kits seem to be delivered within 2 weeks, so if you're quick, you should be able to beat the deadline.  
Although I try to keep politics out of RC2014 discussions, I just want to take this opportunity to apologise to all my EU friends for what is going on right now. I didn't vote for Brexit, and not only am I losing my European freedoms, my EU friends are penalised with tariffs too. Sorry.
