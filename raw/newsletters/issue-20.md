## RC2014 to sponsor OSHCamp 2019

August 31 - September 1 2019 will be my fourth [OSHCamp](http://oshug.org/event/oshcamp2019) event, the second one which I have been a speaker at, and the first one I have sponsored.  This really is a great event that's grown from the Open Source hardware Users Group and is part of the [Wuthering Bytes](https://wutheringbytes.com/) tech festival at Hebden Bridge.  This looks like it has a great lineup again this year, and all attendees will get some RC2014 goodies in their goody bag! Come along if you can!

## Liverpool MakeFest 2019

June 29 is the fifth [Liverpool MakeFest](https://lpoolmakefest.org/), and the RC2014 will be in attendance again this year.  Liverpool MakeFest holds a special place in RC2014 history as this was the first public event that the RC2014 was shown back in 2016. It's a great day out for the whole family, so come along and say Hi if you can!
