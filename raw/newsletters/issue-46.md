## RC2014 Pro Pride and RC2014 Zed Pro Pride

As we head in to June we are celebrating Pride month with two limited edition kits. The [RC2014 Pro Pride](https://z80kits.com/shop/limited-edition-rc2014-pro-pride/) and the[ RC2014 Zed Pro Pride](https://z80kits.com/shop/limited-edition-rc2014-zed-pro-pride/). These are based on the RC2014 Pro and Zed Pro respectively and include extra modules, all of which are colour coded in the Pride Flag livery.   
|  |  ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2046_files/b6d6fd80-c5d0-ce1d-7e84-f9be4b397fa0.jpg)  
---  
Special [dye sublimation processes](https://rc2014.co.uk/2293/dye-sublimation-printing-on-pcbs-for-rc2014-pride/) were developed to ensure the right colours were represented, and every component has been evaluated both for its performance and visual appeal. Take a deeper look over at the [RC2014 website](https://rc2014.co.uk/full-kits/rc2014-pro-pride-and-rc2014-zed-pro-pride/).   
---  
|  ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2046_files/a9265467-70d8-4533-077e-8b6b7e9f0207.jpg)  
---  
**For every kit sold, £20 will be donated to[Trans Rescue](https://transrescue.org/) and £20 will be donated to [Rainbow Railroad](https://www.rainbowrailroad.org/)**. Two fantastic charities that do great work for Trans and LGBTQI folk around the world. Even if you don't buy one of these RC2014 Pride kits, please consider making a donation to them.

## Liverpool MakeFest

RC2014 is back on tour again at one of our favourite events, the [Liverpool MakeFest](https://liverpoolmakefest.org/). This free event is always a good day out for the whole family, so we'd love to see you there on July 1st.   
|   
---  
|  |  ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2046_files/050dbbce-005f-026d-63f8-6ca7f4d5b0f4.jpg)  
---  
The [RP2040 VGA Terminal](https://z80kits.com/shop/rp2040-vga-terminal/) is based on the Pi Pico VGA Terminal but features Micro SD card support, full size USB and a buzzer. All surface mount components are fitted so it only needs connectors soldering on   
---  
|   
---  
|  [ ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2046_files/a5298d67ae67282bc9f53e52c81b3dbe.png) ](https://youtu.be/TTXyYC_mbCs "Port of 3D Monster Maze for RC2014")  
---  
The classic 3D Monster Maze for ZX81 has now been ported to the RC2014 by [Shiela Dixon](https://www.youtube.com/@shieladixon/videos). The code is available to run on a regular 32k RC2014 or an RC2014 Pro, but you will need one of JB Langstons TMS9918A video cards to play it.   
|  |  ![](RC2014%20Newsletter%20-%20Issue%2046_files/845d9fd4-34d7-fc0a-7f9f-a149462de55c.jpg)

## Micro SD Backpack

[PicoTerm](https://github.com/RC2014Z80/picoterm) now has basic SD Card support for the RP2040 VGA Terminal. If you have a Pi Pico VGA Terminal you don't have to miss out. This neat little [Micro SD Backpack](https://z80kits.com/shop/pi-pico-sd-card-backpack/) gives you the same Micro SD card slot and features!
