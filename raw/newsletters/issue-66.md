## Zeds Dead Baby, Zeds Dead 

After almost 8 years, the RC2014 Zed and RC2014 Zed Pro are being retired. These kits have always proved popular, and running RomWBW 3.2.0, and later 3.40, they have been great machines.

## ZID Player for SID-Ulator

If you want to play SID music on your [SID-Ulator](<https://z80kits.com/shop/sid-ulator-sound-module/>), Google Group member [Timothy Blanks](<https://groups.google.com/g/rc2014-z80/c/r72P_iLMbJ4>) has you covered. By converting the 6502 based SID tunes to ZID, a ZID player can play them back on the RC2014 just as nature intended. Looking forward to a large library of tunes soon!

## RIP Ken Boak

It is sad to pass on the news of Ken Boak  (@Monosnite) passing away [Earlier this year](<https://retrocomputingforum.com/t/rip-ken-boak-aka-monsonite/5536>). Ken was a larger than life character and a regular at many of the electronic and retro computer events around the UK. His interest in the RC2014 in the very early days was noted by Hacker News and certainly bought many of you to the platform. You will be missed Ken.

## Kill The Bit - Orton 3C

If you're looking for some fun games to play on your [Orton 3C](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-orton-3c/>), things don't get much better than [Kill The Bit](<https://github.com/harlandski/z80/tree/main/orton-3c/kill-the-bit>). This has been ported across in just 24 bytes by James Harland. His [GitHub](<http://https://github.com/harlandski/z80/tree/main/orton-3c>) repo also has a selection of other code and utilities for the Orton 3C, including a dice simulator that goes up to a D255!
