## RC2014 Powered Birthday Cake

How do you wish the designer of the RC2014 kits a happy birthday? By making a cake that runs from an RC2014 of course! Yes, you read that right, this IMSAI 8080 themed cake made by Shiela Dixon has embedded NeoPixel LEDs that are controlled from an RC2014. It has different lighting patterns and even a Kill The Bit game! After a few bytes, I can confirm that it is a delicious cake too! Read about it on Shielas blog [here](<https://newstuffforoldstuff.blogspot.com/2024/10/im53-8080-birthday-cake.html>)

## Retro Computer Festival 2024

RCF is back again at the [Center For Computing History](<https://www.computinghistory.org.uk/pages/30677/What-s-On/>) in Cambridge. This is always an amazing event and this year is looking to be extra special. There will, of course, be an RC2014 exhibit, along with some kits to buy and even sneaky peeks at upcoming projects.

## 3D Printed Port Covers

If you have a [Rear Panel](<https://z80kits.com/shop/blue-box-rear-panel-kit-110x250x190/>) for the Blue Box enclosure, did you know that you can print your own port covers? Either use the [Thingiverse Configurator](<https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:6791690>) or download the OpenSCAD file from [GitHub](<https://github.com/RC2014Z80/RC2014/blob/master/Hardware/RC2014%20Port%20Cover%20v1.1.scad>)
