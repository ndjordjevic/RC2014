## RC2014 At Hackaday Supercon!

The [Hackaday Supercon](https://hackaday.com/2018/11/08/it-happened-at-supercon-six-days-of-fun-in-a-three-day-con/) in Pasadena is the tech event of the year for a lot of people, and this year it comes with added RC2014 to make it even sweeter!  
  
Not only will Spencer be on a panel during the event, but there will be a free [RC2014 Micro](https://www.tindie.com/products/semachthemonkey/rc2014-micro-single-board-z80-computer-kit) for around 10% of attendees.  
  
If you were lucky enough to get a ticket before they all sold out, make sure you track me down and say Hi!

## Grant Searle Website Move

After a few weeks of uncertainty, [Grant Searle](https://twitter.com/zx80nut) now has a Forever Home for his website at [searle.wales](http://www.searle.wales/)  
Grants site was hugely influential for the RC2014, and is a valuable resource for retro computer fans everywhere. I'm sure a lot of you are breathing a sigh of relief :-)
