## Z80 Kits Shop Launch

  
Exciting news for retro computing fans in the European Union and around the world! To comply with new EU tax laws, RC2014 products are now available at [z80kits.com](https://z80kits.com/)  
  
Since learning that Tindie is unwilling/ unable to comply with the new tax laws, the focus here at RC2014 Towers has been to find a legitimate way to sell kits in to the EU with minimal disruption for customers.  
   
---  
|  [z80kits.com](https://z80kits.com/) has been built to solve this problem. EU taxes are paid at the point of sale and the kits are shipped swiftly via DHL that handle all the paperwork and duty so that there are no unexpected charges or delays for the customers.  
  
For EU customers, [z80kits.com](https://z80kits.com/) is the only place to buy RC2014 kits. We will no longer be shipping to the EU via Tindie.  
  
For the rest of the world, customers can choose between Tindie and [z80kits.com](https://z80kits.com/)
