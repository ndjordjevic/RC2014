# It's Show And Tell Time

The Maker Faire season will be kicking off soon when the RC2014 makes a trip up north to Scotland for the [Edinburgh Mini Maker Faire](http://edinburgh.makerfaire.com/) on 15th April.  Last year it was great fun, and this year is promising to be even better.  
Then a couple of weeks later, RC2014 will be returning to Newcastle for the [UK Maker Faire](https://makerfaireuk.com/) on 28th 29th April.  
As usual, if you are in the vicinity of either of these events, make sure you call in and say hi. You might even be able to pick up a couple of bargains too!

## New ESP8266 WiFi Module 

If you want to control your RC2014 over WiFi, share it's output on the internet, or surf any of the internet based BBS out there, then the [ESP8266 WiFi Module](https://www.tindie.com/products/Semachthemonkey/esp8266-wifi-module-for-rc2014/) is for you!  This kit comes with a pre-programmed ESP8266 ready for Telnet access from your home network. Write your own firmware or use one of the many software images already out there to give it new features.  Even includes a prototyping area to allow further customisation
