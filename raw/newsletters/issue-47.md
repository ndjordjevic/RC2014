## RC2014 Pro Pride and RC2014 Zed Pro Pride

The response to the [RC2014 Pro Pride](https://z80kits.com/shop/limited-edition-rc2014-pro-pride/) and the[ RC2014 Zed Pro Pride](https://z80kits.com/shop/limited-edition-rc2014-zed-pro-pride/) limited edition kits was amazing with the first batch of 20 kits selling out within 3 hours! A total of 69 kits were sold in June, which **raised £2780 for charity**. This was split £1380 each for [Trans Rescue](https://transrescue.org/) and [Rainbow Railroad](https://www.rainbowrailroad.org/) which I know will make a big difference for the people they help.   
|  All of the RC204 Pro Pride kits are sold out, but at time of writing there are still 4 [RC2014 Zed Pro Pride](https://z80kits.com/shop/limited-edition-rc2014-zed-pro-pride/) kits left in stock.  
  
There are still some coloured LEDs and switches left, so these will be listed on z80kits in the next week or two for those that want to add some bling to their less colourful RC2014
