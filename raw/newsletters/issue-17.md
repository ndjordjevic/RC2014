## Maker Faire Wellington

Wellington hosted New Zealands first ever [Maker Faire](https://wellington.makerfaire.com/) at the start of November, and what an awesome Maker Faire it was! It was great to see what Antipodean hackers and makers had to show off.  
  
The RC2014 stall caused a lot of interest with the locals, and lots of faire visitors recounted their early days programming their Zid Ix Eighty One ;-)  
  
The RC2014 stall must have impressed the judges too as I was awarded my first ever "Maker of Merit" ribbon. I'm really thrilled about that!

## Syntax Demo Party

The Syntax Demo Party in Melbourne was a real trip back to the demo scene of the 90s with people getting Commodore C64, Atari 800, Vectrex  and other hardware performing amazing visual and audio tricks that seems impossible on that hardware.  
The RC2014 made a much less impressive ASCII Art animation of a [monkey](https://www.instagram.com/p/BqBiA7LHE-b/?utm_source=ig_share_sheet&igshid=rc9gvhhu4ywo) ! Totally awesome weekend and very inspiring.

The last few weeks has seen RC2014 Meetups held in Wellington, Auckland, Melbourne and Sydney, along with visits to hackerspaces in Melbourne and Brisbane. These events have been really fantastic, and a unique opportunity to get to know you all better.
