## Ukrainian Fundraiser II

Last month saw a donation of £1940 ($2550 USD) paid to the Humanitarian Assistance Fund through the National Bank of Ukraine.  This is thanks to the 40 of you that bought the limited edition [RC2014 Classic II](https://z80kits.com/shop/ukraine-red-cross-fundraiser-rc2014-classic-ii/) kits and [RC2014 Zed](https://z80kits.com/shop/ukraine-red-cross-fundraiser-rc2014-zed/) kits in the colours of the Ukrainian flag.  
  
Demand for these kits was really high and a lot of you asked if there will be another batch of them.  Well, there are just enough parts left over for a very short run of 7 kits (1 RC2014 Classic II and 6 RC2014 Zed).  This will raise another £410 for the Humanitarian Assistance Fund.  
  
**These will go on sale at[18:00 GMT](http://www.timebie.com/std/gmt.php?q=18) tomorrow (13 April 2022)  at [z80kits.com](https://z80kits.com/product-category/fundraiser/) on a first come, first served basis.**

## EMF Camp

  
After cancellation in 2020 for obvious reasons, we are super excited to see the return of [EMF Camp](https://www.emfcamp.org/) this June.  This has always been a fantastic even for the RC2014 community, and this year is shaping up to be another great one.  There will be an RC2014 workshop, as well as a stall at the Night Market in Null Sector, but I'll be around all weekend. So come along and say Hi!
