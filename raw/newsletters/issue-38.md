## Retro Computer Festival 2021!

For the first time in two years, the #RC2014OnTour hashtag is being dusted off as we visit the [Centre for Computing History](http://www.computinghistory.org.uk/) in Cambridge for the Retro Computer Festival on October 9th. Please drop by and say hi if you can. You might even get a sneak preview of some exciting new RC2014 hardware ;-)
