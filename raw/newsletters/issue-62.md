## RC2014 Assembly - 20th September 2025

I am very excited to announce that I have been working with [The National Museum Of Computing](<https://www.tnmoc.org/>) and Peter Onion to bring you the inaugural RC2014 Assembly. This one day event will showcase some of the finest RC2014, RCBus, Z50 and other modern Z80 computers showing off the hardware, software and cool ways that they are used. It is a chance to meet others in the RC2014 community and chat with some of the people behind the kits. The RC2014 Assembly will be hosted by TNMOC at their historic Bletchley Park location and the ticket price includes full access to the museum too.   
|  |  [ ![](https://mcusercontent.com/9794c5b78f76285b2bc1ff7bd/images/861c92ee-b918-46be-af3d-0b6a851d48aa.png) ](<https://www.tindie.com/products/shieladixon/tmsemu3-graphics-card-designed-for-rc2014/>)  
---  
Tickets for exhibitors and traders are limited, but if you have something you would like to show off, kits for sale, or just want to talk about something cool you have done with your RC2014 then I would love to see you there. Feel free to [contact me](<mailto:spencer@z80kits.com?subject=RC2014%20Assembly>) if you want to discuss your ideas.  
  
Tickets go on sale tomorrow on [Eventbrite](<http://https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/rc2014-assembly-tickets-1444643490569>)

So good to meet up with everybody there. Thanks to Tony and Pete for organising the event and to everyone that came along. ## Picasso Stock 

Stock of the [RC2014 Mini II Picasso](<https://z80kits.com/shop/rc2014-mini-ii-picasso/>) is starting to run low, and when they're gone, they're gone. Only a few of each colour left right now, so grab one while you can.  
  
After the last kit is sold I will be writing a blog post highlighting all the details, Easter eggs and secrets hidden within the design, so you can look forward to that coming soon.

## Tracker software for RC2014

Tracker software isn't just used for sending all of your data back to Google! This particular [AY/YM Tracker software](<http://peacockmedia.software/RC2014/MIDI/AYTracker.zip>) from Peacock Media is used for creating music with your YM2149 or AY-3-8910 sound card. [[Or emulated sound card](<https://z80kits.com/shop/why-em-ulator-sound-module/>)!]  
See and hear it in action on the [MIDI IN](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FD9HDzbQ91s>) YouTube channel
