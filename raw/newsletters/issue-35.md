I know some of you are big fans of the RC2014 Easter Egg Hunt. Well, the Easter Bunny has been back to visit RC2014 Towers, and has laid eggs all over the place. ## Get well soon Kim!

Kim Le Brocqu, founder of the RC2014 User Group on Facebook, and all round good guy, is currently going through a hell of a time. In February he was admitted to hospital with Covid Pneumonia and various other complications. He is still in hospital and has since suffered a stroke. On behalf of the RC2014 community, we all wish you a speedy recovery Kim!
