## Landing page — The CP/M Primer (Internet Archive)

Internet Archive scan of *The CP/M Primer* (second edition) by Stephen M. Murtha (and Mitchell Waite). A beginner-oriented introduction to the CP/M operating system — covering CP/M concepts, the command structure, files and disks, built-in and transient commands, and getting productive on a CP/M machine. A classic reference relevant to running CP/M on Z80 systems such as the RC2014. Full text available on archive.org; deep-dive (chapter-level extraction) deferred.
