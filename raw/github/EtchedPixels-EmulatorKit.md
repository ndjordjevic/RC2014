## README (excerpt — brief/index capture)
EmulatorKit is a kit of emulators primarily focused on the RC2014 environment and some Retrobrew (formerly N8VEM) systems. The emulators target software development and testing rather than cycle-accurate simulation; designed to be easy to hack on and simple to understand. Focus reflects RC2014 and Fuzix.

Processors supported include: 1802, 6303, 6502, 65C02, 65C816, 6800, 68000/10/EC020/020 (Musashi), 6803, 6809, 68HC11, 8008, 8080, 8085, 8086, NS8060, NS8070, NS32K, Z8, Z80 (libz80), Z180, Z280.

RCbus / RC2014 / BP80 processor cards emulated: Z80, Z180, Z280, 1802, 6303/6803, 6502, 65C816, 68008, 6808, 6809, 68HC11, 80C85, 80C188, NS32K, TMS9995, Z8.

Integrated systems emulated (RCbus/BP80): SC108, SC114, SC126/30 and relatives, SC720; plus systems with RCbus connector (Easy Z80, T68KRC, MB020, Z280RC, Z80MB64, Z80SBC64).

Official RC2014 card emulation: 512K ROM 512K RAM, Compact Flash, Dual serial SIO/2, IDE (82C55 PPIDE), Pageable ROM, Real Time Clock, Switchable ROM, 64K RAM. Other RCbus cards: 16x50, CTC, EF9345, Floppy, Gluino/Z80 PIO with SD, NMI trap, PS/2 keyboard, TMS9918A, W5100, Z180CoPro, Z80 SIO, ZXKey.

Also emulates Retrobrew/N8VEM systems (SBC v2, Mark IV, etc.), Grant Searle's 9-chip Z80, Nascom 1/2/3, and many other vintage machines.
