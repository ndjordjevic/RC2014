# Starting baseline — RC2014 Micro, schematic-first

The learner owns physical RC2014 machines and wants to understand the **RC2014 Micro** from the
schematic up, hardware-first, to eventually build and modify it. Same approach and rigor as the
parallel MEGA65 learning project (grounded, cited, concrete-first, short lessons).

Prior knowledge not yet probed in detail; the MEGA65 mission states a background comfortable with
**digital logic / electronics** (gates, registers, datasheets) — assume that transfers here unless
shown otherwise. Pitch above absolute-beginner on logic/circuits; introduce Z80- and RC2014-specific
detail from scratch.

Lesson 01 is "reading the RC2014 Micro schematic" (vocabulary + component inventory + the 9 functional
blocks). This record sets the floor; revise once the learner demonstrates (or disclaims) Z80/bus knowledge.
