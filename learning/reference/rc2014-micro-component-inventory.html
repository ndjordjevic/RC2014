RC2014 Micro — Component Inventory

Every part on the schematic (Rev 1.1, March 2019), grounded in assets/RC2014-Micro-schematic.pdf. Quick-reference companion to Lesson 01.

Integrated circuits (U)

RefPartWhat it isBlock
U4Z80 CPU8-bit processor — the brain; drives address bus, moves data, emits control signalsCPU
U3HM62256 (62256)32K×8 static RAM — read/write memoryMemory
U627C51264K×8 EPROM — firmware ROM; A13–A15 select an 8K bankMemory / ROM bank
U2MC68B50ACIA serial UART — the console serial portSerial I/O
U174LS32 (quad OR)Glue logic — OR gates U1A/U1B/U1C combine RD/WR/MREQ and the high address lines (exact wiring traced in the decode lesson)Address decode
U1074LS32 (OR)Glue logic — part of the ROM/RAM chip-select decode (traced in the decode lesson)Address decode
U574HCT04 (hex inverter)Six inverters: U5E+U5F = clock oscillator; U5B → IORQ; U5A/U5C/U5D used/spare in address decode (exact roles traced in the decode lesson)Clock + decode

Variant: on the RC2014 Micro+, U6 is a 39SF010 (128K flash) rather than the 27C512 — everything else here is common.

Serial header & jumpers

RefPartWhat it isBlock
P16-pin header (CONN_01x06)FTDI serial connector — RX, TX, GND, +5 V, with CLK available (console at 115200 8-N-1)Serial I/O
R3, R4ResistorsSeries resistors on the RX/TX serial lines (plus two unlabelled series R at the header)Serial I/O
JP1Jumper (2-pin)Option jumper at the serial headerSerial I/O
JP2, JP3, JP43-pin jumpers (JUMPER3, "ROM Bank Selection")Tie ROM A13/A14/A15 high or low → choose which 8K page of the 64K EPROM runs. Their select nets are labelled S1/S2/S3 on the sheet.ROM bank select
